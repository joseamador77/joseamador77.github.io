FIRST Robotics Competition Team 670 - Sunnnyvale, CA

The FIRST Robotics competition is a high school robotics competition that combines the excitement of sport with the rigors of science and technology. Each year brings a new challenge and teams have six weeks to design, prototype, build, and test 120lb robots to compete with other teams from across the world. I was a part of Team 670 - Homestead Robotics for 4 years throughout high school, eventually becoming the president of the team my senior year. Homestead Robotics Website

Since I was a high schooler, documentation from this chapter of my life is pretty sparse. However, this competition built my foundation and passion for STEM, ultimately inspiring me to pursue a degree in Mechanical Engineering. Here a couple photos and videos that I managed to dig up. Enjoy!
