Process Engineering Intern

I designed a process capability study to improve the manufacturing time of a new product. I conducted a set of experiments with a temperature and humidity chamber that ultimately reduced the adhesive curing time from 90 minutes to 25 minutes, while still maintaining the adhesive properties.

I learned Shure's main manufacturing processes to complete two tasks. First, I created comprehensive instructional documents that would enable new employees to become familiar with a machine in less than an hour. Second, I created physical prototypes of new products for project management.

I became familiar with all types of lasers including marking lasers, welding lasers, and laser cutting. With this knowledge, I was able to create laser marking programs to serialize both new and old products to improve product traceability.
